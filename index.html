Steps to Approach Creation

  • Step 1: Come up with an idea that interests you
  • Step 2: Gain knowledge, insight, and inspiration to gain traction
  • Step 3: Create an approach to accomplish that idea
  • Step 4: Think about how your going to take that approach
  • Step 5: Create the basics
  • Step 6: Dive into complexity
  • Step 7: Touch up and conclude

The Self Hurdle

After looking at your accomplishment you may think "This does not look like much."
Getting over those initial thoughts are hard at first, but it is important to kno that you are striving for greater.

SEE IT THROUGH

The most important thing is to not give up or sell yourself short so to speak.
Part of becoming a developer is trial and error, failing, and trying again.
It's ok to feel overwhelmed, overcoming those insecurites makes it all the more sweeter!
